# Editor quickstart

Welcome. Everything on the site is editable from one place.

## How to log in

Go to **`https://pull-up-neighbor.vercel.app/studio`** (or your custom domain + `/studio`) and sign in with the email that was invited.

## What you'll see

The left sidebar has three sections:

- **Pages** — every page on the site (Home, About, Vision, Programs, Impact, Services, Partners, Contact). Click a page to edit its hero and call-to-action sections. The Home page also lets you edit every section in the middle.
- **Programs / Services / Partner Types / Focus Areas / Stats** — reusable cards. Edit a Program once, and it updates on every page that shows it.
- **Site Settings** — site-wide info.

## How to edit text

Click any field, change the text, click **Publish** in the bottom-right. Changes appear on the live site within a few seconds.

## How to swap the hero video

1. Open **Pages → Home**.
2. Scroll to **Hero**.
3. Click the **Background Video** field → upload a new MP4.
4. (Optional) Update the **Video Poster Image** — this is what users see for the half-second before the video plays, and what people on slow connections see instead of the video.
5. Click **Publish**.

**Important: keep the video small.** Aim for **under 50 MB**. If your file is larger, compress it for free at [handbrake.fr](https://handbrake.fr) — choose the "Web" preset. Big videos make the site load slowly on phones.

## How to highlight words in green

In any **Headline**, fill in the **"Words to highlight in brand green"** field with the exact words you want green (e.g. `Capital`, `Culture`). They'll appear in brand green on the page automatically.

For section titles on the Home page that have a green portion (like "More Than a Nonprofit. **A Platform for Change.**"), wrap the green part with pipe characters: `More Than a Nonprofit. |A Platform for Change.|`

## Publishing

The orange/blue **Publish** button in the bottom-right pushes your changes to the live site. The site updates within ~5 seconds.

## Mistakes happen

Every published version is saved. To revert, click the **clock icon** at the top-right of any document → pick a previous version → click **Restore**.

## Got stuck?

Re-read this guide. If something still seems broken, contact your developer with a screenshot and the URL of the page in Studio.
